Society of Women (SWE) Engineers Team Tech Project

Research Team Lead

In September of 2016, SWE Team Tech partnered with John Deere to create an automatic hitching system. Over the course of the project I was able to fulfill several roles. I served as a research team lead for one of our mechanical systems. I was able to manage a multidisciplinary team, identify our skillsets and deficiencies and come up with a plan to eliminate those deficiencies so that we could best develop our designs. After completion of the mechanical system I lead the displays team. I developed a Pygame system which implemented triangulation methods and provided visuals to guide the tractor operator. We also built a Raspberry Pi prototype which we used to test our systems.

In November of 2017 we presented this project at the SWE national conference with several teams from other universities. Our team was awarded first place!

September 2016 - November 2017
